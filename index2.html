Innovative In Nature

Hi! My name is Oluwatosin Philip Adebayo, I am a
business developer and data analyst. My interest lies in
helping entrepreneurs, businesses, and organisations
achieve their business goals through data-driven market
insights and customer engagements. In my free time, I
volunteer for communities & events that promote good
health and well-being, industrialization, and sustainable
economic growth.

My love for business started in my first year at the
university. I noticed that students find it difficult to
access basic consumer goods and I wanted to create a
solution, so I started a business, Mr. PAT Global
Enterprise, to sell these goods to students in my block.
Years later, the business is now PAT Enterprise with a
focus on customer support, talent management, and,
business development.

I have always loved developing and scaling businesses.
Let me do the same with your business, whether it is
small scale, medium or a large organisation.

My delivery is streamlined

Understand the business task

I ask questions from business owners, or managers
to make sure we are on the same page with the
objective of our work together. Here, I get to
understand the direction you want me to go, and I
spell out the deliverables.

Research everything

Here is where I carry out research to know the
current state of your business/organisation
regarding; strengths, weaknesses, opportunities,
and threats. This process is usually very thorough
as it gives me the growth projection in the industry
or space you are operating.

Analyse for business leads

Based on the outcome of the research, I analyse
the findings to draw data-driven insights that will
connect you to new business opportunities you can,
choose from.

Communicate the business strategy

I enjoy this process because I get to share the
results with you or your stakeholders. I use high
level presentation slides through storytelling to
demonstrate your next winning business strategy..

Implementation

What is the essence of having a winning business
strategy without implementing it? I like to propose
an actionable timeline to management or senior
executives. Here is where you decide what your
measure of success will be as you implement for
your business growth.
